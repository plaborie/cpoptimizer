In the revised version, the authors carefully (and satisfactorily) addressed all the comments I had on the original version. 
I only have minor comments about the CP method:

- CP Optimizer does not really use the Constraint-Based local search approach described in (Hentenryck and Michel 2009). The main ideas of the search algorithm of CP Optimizer are described in [1]. Note that a more detailed article describing CP Optimizer (in particular covering the modeling concepts and the automatc search) will be published in the Constraint journal [2].

[1] Laborie P. and Godard D. 2007. Self-adapting large neighborhood search: application to single-mode scheduling problems. In: Proceedings of MISTA-07, pp 276â€“284 
 
[2] Laborie P., Rogerie J., Shaw P. and Vilim P. 2018. IBM ILOG CP Optimizer for Scheduling. In: Constraints journal. To appear.
